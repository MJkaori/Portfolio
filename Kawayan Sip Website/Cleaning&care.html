How to care for your bamboo straws

RINSE & DRY

1. RINSE, BRUSH & RINSE AGAIN
2. LEAVE TO DRY FULLY
3. STORE IN A DRY, VENTILATED PLACE
The straws can be cleaned in a professional dishwasher, by hand, or boiled for 10 minutes to comply with hygienestandards.

IMPORTANT TIPS:

1.Always let your bamboo straws dry fully. They dry very quickly!
2.Dry them flat – not standing up – so that water does not collect at the bottom.
3.Store your straws in a well-ventilated place, and never in air-tight containers or jars.
After a while you may notice discoloration of the bamboo. This is a natural process and it means that the bamboo straw needs to be replaced.
Usually, with good care, this happens after approximately 4 months.

AFTER USE CLEANING:

1.Submerge the straw(s) in cold or lukewarm water.
2.Use the supplied cleaning brush to clean inside the brush(es).
3.Rinse with clean water, make sure all the soap is out of the bamboo straw
4.Leave to dry in a ventilated place (outside of the cupboard). If you have an espresso machine you can dry them on top of the machine.

DEEP CLEANING:

1.Bring a pot of water to a boil. Then turn off the heat.
2.Soak the straws for a 10-20 minutes.
3.Remove the straws from the water and make sure all the moisture is out of the straws.
4.Leave to dry in a ventilated place (outside of the cupboard).
Always let your bamboo straws dry fully from both sides. Store them in a dry, ventilated place for optimal benefits.
