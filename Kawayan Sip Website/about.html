Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

We are a group of students who wanted to help our community by promoting eco-friendly products to lessen the problems that is also caused by humans that we are facing today.

Our goal is to develop an eco-friendly community for the generations behind us. to educate people what is good and bad for our community.
We wanted to be the voice of our nature to bring awareness to the community. We wanted change for better.

Straws are only the beginning

Here in the Philippines, plastic is one of the biggest problem. Plastic pollution is caused by the accumulation of plastic waste in the environment. It can be categorized in primary plastics, such as bottle cups, cigarette butts, straw etc. Plastic has a good qualities, ease the shaping, low cost, mechanical resistance, etc. Being the ideal material for packaging, plastic is basically everywhere.

But what are the causes? Negligence is the main cause. Because of poorly recycled, dumped in the landfill or abandoned in nature. This waste is carried by winds, pushed by the rains into sewers, streams, rivers and finally in the oceans. Natural disaster such as floods must be considered as other causes of plastic pollution as well.

When this kind of problem can't be solved immediately, it will cause more floods, more waste on the oceans, the animals will die, harmful effect on the environment and human as well. While doing the 3R's, if we started using our product it can also lessen the problem, which is eco-friendly and made of bamboo which is can be found in the Philippines with 60 bamboo species.

Through our website and social media, the people who visit our website and social media can be aware about the serious problem about plastic.
